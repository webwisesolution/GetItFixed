Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are done within the same day. Complex issues might take 1-2 days, but we'll always keep you informed.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes – between 30 and 90 days depending on service. All warranties are clearly stated on your repair receipt.

Is diagnosis free?

Absolutely. We provide free diagnosis for all devices so you know exactly what's wrong before committing to any repair.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer convenient pickup and delivery services within Nairobi for a small fee. Contact us to arrange this service.

Our Story

GetItFixed was founded in 2018 by Allan, a passionate tech enthusiast with a vision to provide affordable, reliable device repair services to the Nairobi community.

What started as a small repair booth in Kimathi House has grown into a trusted repair center serving thousands of satisfied customers across Nairobi.

Our mission is simple: to extend the life of your devices through quality repairs, reducing electronic waste and saving you money.
